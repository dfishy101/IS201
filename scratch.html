Some Cool Pickleball Rules

  1. Round-Robin + Knockout Format
    • All teams play every other team in quick first-to-9-point games
    • The top 4 teams will then go to the semi-finals
  2. "Third-Shot Must-Drop" Rule
    • The third shot after the serve must be a drop shot landing in the kitchen
    • This then encourages strategic play rather than slamming the ball
  3. "Golden Point" Rule
    • At 10-10 in any game, you enter in sudden death where the next point wins
    • Serve will go to the team who didn't serve the last point
